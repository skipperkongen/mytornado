What we mean by the words «eco-friendly online advertising»:

  • Promotions for products like organic food, organic t-shirts, electic cars, sustainable vacations etc
  • Promotions for events such as festivals, conventions, workshops etc, as long as they have an eco-friendly theme
  • And by eco-friendly we also mean sustainable, green, organic, dreadlocky, vegan, native, having good karma and saving the planet.
Sure, it is rather subjective what eco-friendly means, but we think that choice is better left to humans (such as you) than to robots living in a datacenter :-)

For eco-friendly advertisers

We show your ads on websites, give you stats and insight about how users respond to your products, let you manage your campaigns by territory etc. You know, the usual deal. So what's new?

  • We show your ads to people who are passionate about everything eco-friendly!
  • This means you will have more clicks per ad impression
  • You can advertise for free on Greenwords if your project is both non-profit and eco-friendly!
  • Greenwords offers cheap prices to eco-friendly commercial advertisers, because you are doing your part for a healthy planet. We want to help you!

For eco-friendly website owners

You earn money every time an advertisement is shown on your website, and you earn even more when a user click an ad. You know, the usual deal. So what's new?

  • General online advertisement providers such as Google ultimately decide what ads to display on your website. Sometimes those ads areill-aligned with your values and the values you promote on your website. You basically have very little control over the advertisement content
  • Greenwords will only show 100% eco-friendly advertisements on your website, end of story!
  • We know it's not all about money. You may choose to let a percentage of the ads be for non-profit projects that make a difference for the future of our planet! Think of this as a kind of charity that you can opt in on :-)
